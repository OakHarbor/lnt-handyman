LNT Handyman Construction

Experienced home renovators for Oak Harbor & Whidbey Island

My name is Les Higgins, I reside here on Whidbey Island in Washington State for as long as I can remember. I have been in the Labors Union for over 20 years working with contractors on countless projects and construction sites doing everything from complete demolition to starting a project from the foundation and up. In those 20 years I think I can confidently say I have seen almost anything when it comes to commercial and residential construction. Even if I haven't seen it, I know I have the right experience and know-how to get the job done.

After 20 years I decided it was time to get into business for myself and continue to do what I love, but as my own boss and company. Since 2015 LNT Handyman Construction has been providing Whidbey Island with the best service and best quality work as a general contractor. I love what I do and I hope I get the chance to share my craft with you and get that project done you swore to yourself you were going to finish last year.
